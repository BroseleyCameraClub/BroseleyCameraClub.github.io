Welcome to Broseley Camera Club's Website



We are a friendly bunch of like minded people, who get together and discuss as many aspects of photography as we can. We meet only once a month, having a break in August and December. The meetings are very informal and easy to follow.

We have workshops and presentations that suit all abilities, from beginners to advanced, phone to DSLR. There really is something for everyone. Please read through our 'About Page' for more about our club.
